* Context
`futon3.cue-embedding` projects Tatami intent and sigil cues into fixed-length
vectors. It tokenizes intent text, scores overlaps against pattern summaries,
then derives fruit/paramita cues from the winning sigils. `embed-cues` converts
those cue scores into ordered vectors so downstream systems can compare or
store them consistently.

When no explicit patterns exist, the module falls back to a local corpus of
fruit/orb descriptions to synthesize minimal sigil candidates. `annotate-entry`
is the public helper that attaches cue embeddings and intent cues onto a Tatami
entry map.
