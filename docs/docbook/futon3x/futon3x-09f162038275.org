* Trajectory computation

A trajectory compares two time windows of equal length:

- *Baseline window:* =now - 2N days= to =now - N days=
- *Current window:* =now - N days= to =now=

The trajectory delta captures:

** Freedom expansion
- *New functionings:* types present in current but absent from baseline
- *Strengthened:* types that gained reproducibility (became capabilities)
- =freedom-expansion= = count of new + strengthened

** Freedom regression
- *Lost functionings:* types present in baseline but absent from current
- *Weakened:* types that lost reproducibility (ceased being capabilities)
- =freedom-regression= = count of lost + weakened

** Net freedom delta
- =net-freedom-delta= = expansion − regression
- Positive = agent's capability set is growing
- Negative = agent is losing capabilities (may be expected during exploration)

** Discipline delta
Compares PSR, PUR, PAR counts and continuity ratio between windows.

* Baldwin phase detection

The Baldwin cycle (futon-theory/baldwin-cycle 🔃/三) has three phases:

| Phase | Diversity rate | Reproducibility rate | Interpretation |
|-------+----------------+---------------------+----------------|
| Exploring | high (>0.3) | low (<0.5) | Trying many new things, few reproducible yet |
| Assimilating | low (≤0.3) | moderate (0.3–0.7) | Consolidating gains, building reproducibility |
| Canalizing | low (≤0.3) | high (>0.7) | Stable capability set, routinized |
| Mixed | — | — | Neither exploration nor canalization dominant |

** Rates

- *Diversity rate:* proportion of current functioning types that are new
  (not seen in baseline). High diversity = exploring.
- *Reproducibility rate:* proportion of current functioning types that are
  reproducible (≥2 sessions). High reproducibility = canalized.

* Phase-appropriate warnings

The Baldwin phase contextualizes the trajectory. Some patterns are expected
at certain phases but concerning at others:

| Pattern | During exploring | During assimilating | During canalizing |
|---------+------------------+---------------------+-------------------|
| Regression | expected | ⚠ concerning | ⚠⚠ alarming |
| Weakening | expected | ⚠ concerning | ⚠⚠ alarming |
| No new types | concerning | expected | expected |
| Low reproducibility | expected | concerning | ⚠ alarming |

When a phase-inappropriate pattern is detected, the trajectory report
includes ⚠ warnings with specific details (which types were lost/weakened).

This makes the Baldwin phase /not decorative/ — it directly affects how
trajectory deltas are interpreted and reported.
