* Context
The self-representing stack is the capability that lets the futon system describe
its own structure at every level. From a strategic claim in a war bulletin, you
can trace through devmaps, tensions, evidence entries, reflection envelopes, and
arrive at a specific Clojure var in a specific file at a specific line number.

This is what distinguishes the futon3x system from the old futon3 HX mockup.
The mockup could validate asserted links. The self-representing stack derives
structural facts from the live system and traces them to ground truth.

The entries in this section demonstrate the complete chain: reflection grounding,
evidence navigation, the full end-to-end trace, and the theoretical framework
(間 + 関) that names what the trace does.
