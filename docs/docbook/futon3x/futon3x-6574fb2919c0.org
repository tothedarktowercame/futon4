* Context
~POST /api/alpha/portfolio/step~ runs one complete AIF cycle. The loop:

1. *Observe* — scan repos, compute mission inventory, coverage, evidence
   velocity. Normalize to 15 channels (see "Belief state and mu channels").
2. *Perceive* — compute prediction errors: expected channel values (from beliefs)
   minus observed values. This is the free energy.
3. *Affect* — update mode (BUILD/MAINTAIN/CONSOLIDATE) and precision weights
   based on prediction errors. High coverage error sharpens coverage channels.
4. *Policy* — rank candidate actions by expected free energy. Return the action
   that minimizes surprise given current beliefs.

The step emits evidence to the durable store, making the inference process
itself part of the evidence landscape.

The system recommends ~review~ with expected free energy G=-0.300 and 22.4%
probability. The ~tau~ (confidence threshold) is 1.40 — when tau drops below 1.0,
the system abstains rather than making low-confidence recommendations.

The ~structure~ field (not shown above) includes the full portfolio: 72 missions,
24 complete, 24 in-progress, 15 ready, with blocked pairs and critical path.
