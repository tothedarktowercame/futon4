* Context
Bridge triples — warrants for sense-shift hops between meme entities. A bridge
is a (source, target, warrant) triple where the warrant justifies why a
conceptual shift from source to target is valid.

Not heavily used in the current e2e pipeline but part of the meme store's
design for supporting analogical reasoning across pattern domains.
