## Promoted to rubric

**A1-ep12 (pre-existing-community-as-prerequisite for course creation)** is the highest-leverage single addition; it names a structural variable that Peter learned the hard way over 10 years of running COOPS and that the Handbook doesn't make explicit. Bears directly on the carrier-shape decision: any carrier (zine, course, infrastructure) presupposes a community-or-network it's serving — without one, the carrier doesn't deliver learning. Promoted as new rubric item **1.9 Pre-existing community is the upstream variable; course-creation (or carrier-creation more generally) without an existing community to serve produces abandoned artifacts, not learning.**
